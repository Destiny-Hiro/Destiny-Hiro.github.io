Dice Roller

Over the past few weeks, I made this for my CSCI 4208 class, it was alot of fun to make dice and creating a small game for someone to play in the meantime. It was made based on my love of dice games and I wanted to have nice style so I chose transparent images of dice and a cool, blue gradient for the background. There were many more game ideas I can make now that I have this as a base to build off of, and it has me inspired to continue beyond what I have currently.
